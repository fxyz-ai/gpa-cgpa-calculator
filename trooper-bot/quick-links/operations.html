OPERATIONS

The Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) is one of the largest paramilitary forces in the world, primarily responsible for internal security and counter-insurgency operations in India. CRPF personnel are involved in various operations across the country, including but not limited to:

Counter-Insurgency Operations

CRPF plays a crucial role in countering insurgencies and dealing with left-wing extremism (Maoist/Naxalite) in affected regions of India. They conduct anti-insurgency operations in states like Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Bihar, and Odisha.

Counter-Terrorism Operations

CRPF is often deployed in counter-terrorism operations in conflict-prone areas such as Jammu and Kashmir. They work in coordination with the local police and other security forces to combat terrorism and maintain law and order.

Riot Control and Crowd Management

CRPF is frequently called upon to control riots and manage large crowds during protests, demonstrations, and public gatherings to ensure peace and order is maintained.

Border Security

In addition to its internal security role, CRPF also assists the Border Security Force (BSF) in guarding India's international borders, particularly in areas where there is a risk of infiltration or smuggling.

Election Security

CRPF personnel are often deployed during elections to ensure a peaceful and secure voting process. They help maintain law and order and provide security at polling stations.

Disaster Relief

CRPF is involved in disaster relief operations during natural disasters such as earthquakes, floods, and cyclones. They provide assistance in rescue and relief efforts.

VIP Security

CRPF also provides security to high-profile individuals, including politicians and visiting dignitaries.

Specialized Units

CRPF has specialized units like the Rapid Action Force (RAF) that are trained and equipped for rapid deployment in critical situations such as communal riots and other emergencies.

Training

The CRPF also conducts training programs for its personnel and other law enforcement agencies in areas like counter-terrorism, jungle warfare, and disaster response.
